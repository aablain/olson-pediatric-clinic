Appointment and Scheduling Information

We are accepting new patients from birth to age 18.

Our staff is here to assist you in scheduling of wellness exams, non-urgent appointments and same day sick visits. If you are unsure if your child needs to be seen right away, an RN will return your call and advise you. Phone lines open at 8:15 AM.

We are in the office Monday-Friday, weekends and holidays. Extended evening hours are available for your sick child.

Routine Well-Child Visits

Please schedule routine well-child visits several weeks in advance, making sure the appointment is scheduled on or after the childâ€™s appropriate age for the visit. Cancellations need to occur 24 hours in advance, allowing another child to take the visit time.

Urgent Care

Being available for our patients with urgent, non-emergency medical needs is integral to our comprehensive care commitment. Sick visits that need to be seen on a same-day basis should be called in as early as possible. We will do our best to schedule with your primary pediatrician, but if they are not available we can schedule you with one of our other doctors.
