Billing and Insurance

Billing

Our billing office is open from 8:00 AM- 5:00 pm Monday-Friday. The department is open and accessible, and the staff is available to answer any questions about your account and insurance/

Insurance

We recommend that you are aware of your insurance coverage and specific benefits, limitations and financial responsibilities. With thousands of insurance companies and benefit plans, we are unable to know the details of each individual plan. We attempt to provide care within your insurance benefit allowances, however; our primary concern is to recommend the medically appropriate care for your child.

As a courtesy to our patients, we will bill your insurance upon receipt of a current insurance card. To assure maximum benefits, please bring your insurance card to every visit. Often, important numbers change, so inform us if you receive a new card, and whether it is from a new company or the same one.

Co-pays are due at the time of service. This is an agreement between you and your insurance company. We accept cash, personal checks, and most major credit cards for co-pays and account payments.

If you are without insurance and would like to pay for your visit the same day, we offer a 15% discount.

Please review our Financial and Credit Policy, on the Forms page. If you have questions about any of our financial policies please contact our billing department.

Referrals

Many insurance plans require referral or authorizations. Please contact our referral department at 503-635-3743, prior to having services performed.
