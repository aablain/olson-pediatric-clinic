Sick Child

Our Acute Care Clinic is open 365 days a year.

If your child is sick or has sustained an injury, and you require medical advice a pediatric registered nurse will answer your questions and offer recommendations, including making a same day appointment if needed. We will do our best to schedule with your primary pediatrician, but if they are not available we can schedule you with one of our other doctors. If you do need an urgent same day appointment, please call as soon as possible, to ensure your child is seen in a timely manner, saving you a trip to the Emergency Department or Urgent Care Center. Lab is available on site, for any injuries or illness that may require lab work.
