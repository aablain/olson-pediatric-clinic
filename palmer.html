Dr. Donald Palmer

Dr. Donald Palmer founded Olson Pediatric Clinic in 1972. He attended University of Illinois Medical School and did his pediatric residency at Tulane University in New Orleans, and University of Virginia, Charlottesville. Dr. Palmer is a member of the Academy of Pediatrics, the North Pacific Pediatric Society, and the Oregon Pediatric Society.

Dr. Palmer and his wife, Joan, enjoy spending time at the beach. They have two grown children and a dog. Dr. Palmer retired at the end of 2013.
